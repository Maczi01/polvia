# Cookie policy

Here you will find information about the Cookies Policy provided by the platform Pol.ie. This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you browse websites. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and provide personalized content. Cookies may be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (remain until they expire or are deleted).

## How we use cookies

- **Essential cookies**: Necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., login sessions, security features). These cannot be disabled.
- **Analytical cookies**: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve performance.
- **Advertising cookies**: Used to deliver personalized ads based on your interests (may track activity across sites).
- **Functional cookies**: Remember your preferences (e.g., language, font size) for a smoother experience.

## Your cookie choices

You can accept or reject cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing ones, or receive alerts when cookies are sent. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

## Third-party cookies

Some third-party services used on our website, such as embedded videos (YouTube), social media widgets (Facebook/Twitter), or analytics tools, may place their own cookies. We do not control these cookies—review the respective third-party policies for more details.

## Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify and update this Cookies Policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
